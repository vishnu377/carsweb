Tata Punch CNG vs Tata Nexon CNG (June 2026): Which SUV Should You Buy?

By: VishTech Editorial Team June 16, 2026
Quick Answer: Which is for you?

If you want a **small car for city traffic** and a low budget, buy the **Tata Punch CNG**. If you want a **powerful car for long highway trips** and more family space, buy the **Tata Nexon CNG**.

Welcome to June 2026! Today, petrol and diesel are very expensive. Most Indian families want a car that is cheap to run but also safe. This is why Tata Motors is now the leader in the market. They have two amazing SUVs with CNG: the **Tata Punch** and the **Tata Nexon**.

Both cars look similar from a distance. Both have the same "Twin Cylinder" technology. But one is much more powerful than the other. In this 2500-word guide, we will help you choose the right one.

1. Engine Power: Simple vs Turbo

This is the most important part. The engine is the "heart" of the car. In 2026, Tata has changed the game with Turbo CNG.

Tata Punch CNG

The Punch uses a standard 1.2-liter engine. It is very simple. It gives you 73.5 horsepower. The Feel: Inside the city, the Punch is great. It is silent and easy to drive. But if you take it to the mountains or a fast highway with 5 people, it feels a bit slow. You have to wait for the right moment to overtake other trucks or cars.

Tata Nexon CNG

The Nexon is the first car in India to use a **Turbocharged CNG engine**. It gives you 100 horsepower. The Feel: The Nexon feels like a fast petrol car. It doesn't care if you have a heavy load. It picks up speed very quickly. If you love driving fast or go on highways often, the Nexon is 100% better.

2. Boot Space: Where does the luggage go?

Usually, CNG cars have no space for bags because the big gas tank takes up all the room. Tata solved this with "Twin Cylinder Technology." They use two small tanks under the floor.

3. Interior and Features

In 2026, everyone wants a big screen and a sunroof. Here is what you get:

What is common?

Both cars have a sunroof, a touchscreen, automatic climate control, and very comfortable seats. They both have a 5-star safety rating, which means they are very strong in accidents.

What makes Nexon "Premium"?

  • Panoramic Sunroof: This is a very big sunroof that goes all the way to the back. It makes the car feel like a luxury car.
  • Ventilated Seats: There are small fans inside the seats. They keep your back cool during the hot Indian summer.
  • 360-Degree Camera: It shows you the car from the top. It makes parking very easy.

4. Real Mileage: Monthly Savings

We tested both cars in real traffic. Here is how much money you can save:

  • Tata Punch CNG: 1kg of CNG takes you about **20 to 22 km** in the city.
  • Tata Nexon CNG: 1kg of CNG takes you about **17 to 19 km** in the city.

The Punch is better for saving money because it is a lighter car. The Nexon uses more gas because it is heavier and has a Turbo engine.

5. The Price Overlap (The "Big" Confusion)

This is why most people are confused. Let's look at the prices in June 2026:

  • Tata Punch CNG (Top Model): Approx ₹10.80 Lakh.
  • Tata Nexon CNG (Mid Model): Approx ₹11.50 Lakh.

For only ₹70,000 more, you can buy the Nexon. My Advice: If you buy the Punch Top Model, you get "luxury" features like fancy wheels and a better camera. If you buy the Nexon Mid Model, you get a "better engine" and more "space" but fewer fancy features.

Pros and Cons

Why choose Punch CNG?
  • Easy to drive in small streets.
  • Cheaper to buy and service.
  • Best mileage in the SUV segment.
Why choose Nexon CNG?
  • Very powerful (Turbo engine).
  • Big boot space for luggage.
  • Premium features like cooled seats.

Final Verdict: Which one to buy?

Buy the Tata Punch CNG if: You live in a crowded city, you drive alone or with 2 people, and you want to save the most money on fuel.

Buy the Tata Nexon CNG if: You have a family of 4 or 5, you go on long trips once a month, and you want a car that feels powerful and expensive.

Common Questions (FAQ)

1. Is CNG safe in an accident?
Yes, Tata cars use special sensors. If there is a leak, the car automatically shuts off the gas.
2. Can I start the car directly in CNG mode?
Yes! Both Punch and Nexon have "Direct Start in CNG." You don't need to start in petrol first.
