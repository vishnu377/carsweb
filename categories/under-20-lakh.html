Buying Guide: Tips for ₹10L - ₹20L Budget

  • Engine Choice: In this budget, you can opt for Strong Hybrids (Grand Vitara/Hyryder) for high city mileage or Turbo Petrols (Slavia/Kushaq) for performance.
  • Safety & Tech: Most cars in this range now offer Level 2 ADAS. Ensure you pick a car with at least 6 airbags as standard.
  • Resale Value: Hyundai and Maruti cars generally offer better resale value in the long run.
