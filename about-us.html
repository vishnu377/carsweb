Our Mission

Making car buying simple for every Indian

Buying a car in India can be overwhelming. Often, buyers receive biased information focused on sales rather than their actual needs. VishTech Cars was founded in Jaipur to solve this exact problem.

Our goal is simple: to provide you with the same honest information a knowledgeable friend would give—without any bias or dealership pressure.

  • Honest Reviews — We highlight both the strengths and the limitations of every car.
  • Real-World Data — Focused on Indian road conditions and fuel efficiency.
  • Free Resources — Access our EMI and Fuel calculators at no cost.
  • Unbiased Content — We do not accept paid promotions from car brands.
