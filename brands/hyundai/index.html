Why Hyundai is Dominating the Tech Space?

Hyundai has consistently set new benchmarks in India with its "First-in-segment" features. Whether it was the introduction of the sunroof in affordable cars or the widespread adoption of Level 2 ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems), Hyundai stays ahead of the curve. With its new 'Creta EV' and 'Ioniq' series, the brand is now focused on becoming the leader in the premium electric vehicle market in India.
