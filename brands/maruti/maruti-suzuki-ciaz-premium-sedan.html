1. Smart Hybrid Performance

The 2025 Ciaz is powered by the 1.5L K15 Petrol Engine. It features the **Smart Hybrid (SHVS)** technology. This system uses a dual-battery setup to help the engine during acceleration, which reduces fuel consumption and improves smoothness.

The engine produces 103 bhp and 138 Nm of torque. While it is not a sports car, it is exceptionally refined and quiet, making it perfect for long highway cruises and city traffic.

2. Unmatched Interior Comfort

The biggest selling point of the Ciaz is its cabin space. It offers the best legroom in its category, rivaling cars from much higher price segments. The dashboard features a clean design with a wood-finish garnish and a 7-inch SmartPlay Pro touchscreen.

  • Premium Seating: High-quality leatherette seats in the Alpha variant.
  • Huge Boot Space: 510 Liters of space for all your luggage.
  • Rear Comfort: Includes rear AC vents and a manual sunshade.

4. Safety: Standard Protection

Maruti has updated the Ciaz with the NEXA Safety Shield. All variants now come with essential safety features to protect you and your family:

  • Dual Airbags: Protection for the driver and co-passenger.
  • ESP & Hill Hold: Prevents the car from sliding on slopes (Standard).
  • ABS with EBD: Better braking control during emergencies.
  • ISOFIX: Dedicated mounts for child seats.

7. Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. Is Ciaz better than Honda City?

Ans. The Honda City offers more power and features, but the Ciaz is more affordable, offers more rear-seat legroom, and has a lower maintenance cost.

Q2. Does Ciaz come with a Diesel engine?

Ans. No, Maruti Suzuki has discontinued all Diesel engines. The Ciaz is now only available with a 1.5L Petrol Smart Hybrid engine.

Q3. Is the Ciaz good for long trips?

Ans. Yes, it is one of the most comfortable sedans for long highway journeys due to its soft suspension and spacious cabin.
