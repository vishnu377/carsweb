Why India Loves Skoda?

Skoda has built a niche for itself by offering German engineering at a competitive price. In 2026, Skoda's focus is on the MQB-A0-IN platform, which ensures that even their most affordable SUV, the Kylaq, doesn't compromise on build quality. With 5-star Global NCAP ratings for Kushaq and Slavia, Skoda remains the top choice for safety-conscious buyers who love the 'TSI' punch and high-speed stability.
