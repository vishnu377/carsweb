Mahindra XUV 3XO: Kya Ye Sach Mein Segment Ki King Hai? Mera Honest Opinion!

By: Auto News Editorial Updated: Jan 3, 2026
"Bhai, ye sunroof hai ya poora aasman!" — Ye mere dost ka pehla reaction tha jab usne XUV 3XO ki Skyroof dekhi. Maine pichle hafte isse ek weekend trip par lonavala le gaya, aur sach bataun toh Mahindra ne is baar game hi palat diya hai. Sirf naam nahi badla, gaadi ki poori 'Vibe' hi badal di hai.

Mahindra XUV 3XO market mein aate hi un logon ki favourite ban gayi hai jo features aur safety ke saath compromise nahi karna chahte. Purani XUV300 ki jo shikayatein thi—jaise chota cabin ya purana dashboard—unhe Mahindra ne is baar jadd se khatam kar diya hai.

Design: Love It or Hate It, You Can't Ignore It!

XUV 3XO ka design kaafi bold hai. Iske front mein jo new LED DRLs hain aur piche ki Infinite LED Tail Lamps hain, wo raat ke waqt kisi premium Audi ya BMW jaisi feel deti hain. Shuruat mein mujhe iska look thoda ajeeb laga tha, par jab aap ise road par chalte hue dekhte hain, toh ye kaafi impressive lagti hai.

Cabin Experience: Luxury Jo Dil Jeet Le

Andar baithte hi aapko sabse pehle feel hogi iski 'Space'. Is segment ki baaki SUVs ke muqable iski width zyada hai, toh piche 3 log bade aaram se baith paate hain.

Mere liye sabse bada highlight iska 10.25-inch Dual Screen setup tha. Music bajane ke liye isme Harman Kardon ka system diya gaya hai, aur trust me, highway par gaane sunne ka mazaa hi kuch aur hai. Haan, wo white interior thoda jaldi ganda hota hai, par premium feel wahi deta hai.

XUV 3XO Quick Highlights:
  • Engine: 1.2L Turbo Petrol / 1.5L Diesel Engine.
  • Transmission: 6-Speed MT / Torque Converter AT.
  • Feature King: First-in-segment Panoramic Sunroof.
  • Safety: 6 Airbags standard aur Level 2 ADAS.

Safety: Mahindra Ka Bharosa

Humne highway par iska Level 2 ADAS test kiya. Adaptive Cruise Control aur Lane Keep Assist itne smoothly kaam karte hain ki drive ka thakawat aadhi ho jati hai. 6 Airbags aur 360-degree camera aapko wo confidence dete hain jo is segment mein koi aur gaadi shayad hi de paye.

Faisla: Kya Aapko Ise Khareedna Chahiye?

XUV 3XO ki shuruati keemat ₹7.49 Lakh se hoti hai aur top model tak jaate-jaate ye features ke maamle mein Creta ko bhi takkar deti hai. Agar aapko ek aisi gaadi chahiye jo dekhne mein modern ho, safety mein solid ho aur jisme sunroof ke naam par pura "Sky" mil jaye, toh aankh band karke 3XO par ja sakte hain.
