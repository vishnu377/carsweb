Which Mahindra SUV Should You Choose?

Mahindra has an SUV for every personality. Here is how to pick yours:

  • For the Off-Road Lover: The Thar Roxx or the classic 3-door Thar. Nothing else comes close to their power on dirt roads.
  • For the Tech-Savvy Family: The XUV700. It offers a premium dual-screen setup and advanced ADAS safety.
  • For the Rugged Boss: The Scorpio N. It is the "Big Daddy of SUVs" with unmatched road presence.
  • For the Budget Explorer: The XUV 3XO. It gives you SUV feels and a sunroof at a very affordable price.

Why India Trusts Mahindra?

Mahindra's journey from building tractors to world-class safety-rated SUVs is a story of Indian engineering excellence. With almost all their new SUVs scoring 5 stars in Global NCAP, Mahindra has become synonymous with safety and toughness. Their commitment to 'Born Electric' (BE) platforms shows that Mahindra is ready to lead the world in the electric SUV revolution.
