The Spirit of Mahindra

Mahindra & Mahindra has a rich heritage of building "Tough" vehicles. From the classic Bolero to the tech-loaded XUV700, Mahindra cars are designed to conquer any terrain. With the launch of the 'Thar Roxx', Mahindra has successfully bridged the gap between a hardcore off-roader and a premium family SUV. Their upcoming 'Born Electric' (BE) range is set to dominate the Indian EV space in the coming years.
