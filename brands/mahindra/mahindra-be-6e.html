Mahindra BE 6e: Is This The Most Futuristic SUV Ever Made in India?

By: VishTechCars - Automotive Blogger Published: Jan 10, 2025
"Wait... is that a car or a spacecraft?" That was my first thought when I saw the Mahindra BE 6e. Honestly, I have seen many luxury EVs, but Mahindra has truly outdone itself this time. If you think Indian cars are only about budget, this SUV will prove you wrong. Let’s dive into what makes this car a game-changer.

The Mahindra BE 6e is not just another electric SUV. It is built on the new "Born Electric" (INGLO) platform, which means it was designed from scratch to be an EV. Forget the old designs; this car looks like it belongs in the year 2030.

Exterior: A Head-Turner Design

The first thing you will notice is the sharp, aggressive look. The signature C-shaped LED DRLs and the aerodynamic body make it look incredibly sleek. During my research, I found that Mahindra focused heavily on air-flow design to increase the battery range. It has a high ground clearance, making it perfect for Indian roads while still looking like a premium sports SUV.

The Cockpit: Triple Screen Magic!

Step inside, and you will feel like a pilot. The BE 6e features a massive Triple Screen Setup—one for the driver, one for navigation/entertainment, and one even for the passenger! This is a feature usually found in high-end luxury cars like Mercedes or BMW.

The gear shifter looks like a joystick, and the ambient lighting creates a futuristic mood. The space inside is huge because there is no engine under the hood, giving you extra legroom and a flat floor.

Mahindra BE 6e Key Highlights:
  • Battery: High-density 60kWh to 79kWh packs.
  • Range: Expected real-world range of 450-500 km per charge.
  • Performance: 0 to 100 km/h in just 6.7 seconds!
  • Technology: Level 2+ ADAS for top-tier safety.

Performance: Speed Meets Efficiency

Electric cars are known for speed, and the BE 6e is a beast. With its powerful motor, it can touch 100 km/h faster than many petrol cars in its segment. It supports Ultra-Fast Charging, allowing you to charge from 5% to 80% in less than 30 minutes at a fast-charging station. This makes long-distance travel very easy.

Price and Launch: When Can You Buy It?

Mahindra is expected to launch the BE 6e at a competitive price point. The estimated starting price could be around ₹18 Lakh to ₹22 Lakh, putting it in direct competition with the Tata Curvv EV and Hyundai Creta EV.

My Verdict: If you want a car that makes people stop and look, and you love high-end technology, the Mahindra BE 6e should be on your list. It is bold, fast, and 100% futuristic.
