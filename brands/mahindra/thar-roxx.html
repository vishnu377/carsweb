Mahindra Thar Roxx: The Ultimate 5-Door Family Off-Roader is Here

By: Auto News Editorial Last Updated: Dec 31, 2025

Mahindra has finally launched the much-awaited Thar Roxx, a 5-door version of the legendary Thar, designed to be the perfect family SUV without compromising on off-road DNA.

The Mahindra Thar Roxx is not just a longer Thar; it is a complete evolution. With a longer wheelbase, premium interiors, and a host of modern features, it targets urban families who love weekend adventures. From a panoramic sunroof to Level 2 ADAS, the Roxx is packed with technology.

Exterior: Bold and Rugged

The Thar Roxx features a new 6-slot grille, circular LED headlamps with integrated C-shaped DRLs, and 19-inch diamond-cut alloy wheels. The extra pair of doors makes ingress and egress much easier for rear passengers, making it a practical choice for families.

Interior and Comfort

Step inside, and you'll find a white-themed premium cabin. The Thar Roxx comes with a 10.25-inch touchscreen infotainment system, a fully digital instrument cluster, and a premium 9-speaker Harman Kardon sound system. Ventilated seats and leatherette upholstery add a touch of luxury rarely seen in off-road vehicles.

Price and Variants

The Mahindra Thar Roxx is competitively priced, starting from ₹12.99 Lakh for the entry-level RWD petrol variant, going up to ₹22.49 Lakh for the top-end 4x4 diesel variant (ex-showroom). It competes directly with the Force Gurkha 5-door and mid-size SUVs like the Hyundai Creta and Kia Seltos.
