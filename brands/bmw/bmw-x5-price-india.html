The BMW X5 is often called "The Boss" of luxury SUVs. It is big, powerful, and very comfortable. But with many new SUVs launching in India, is the X5 still worth your money?

In this post, I will explain everything about the BMW X5 2026 model—from its high-tech features to its engine performance. If you are planning to buy a luxury SUV, this guide is for you!

2. Engine and Power

BMW offers two very powerful 3.0-liter 6-cylinder engines. Both engines are smooth and come with an 8-speed automatic gearbox.

  • Petrol (xDrive40i): 381 Horsepower | 0-100 km/h in 5.4 seconds.
  • Diesel (xDrive30d): 286 Horsepower | 650 Nm Torque (Very powerful for climbing hills).
  • All-Wheel Drive: The xDrive system sends power to all four wheels for better grip.

3. Design and Road Presence

The 2026 BMW X5 looks very modern. It has sharp LED headlights and a large front grille that can light up at night (called the Iconic Glow grille). The M Sport version looks even more aggressive with bigger wheels and sporty bumpers.

4. Ground Clearance (Practicality)

For Indian roads, ground clearance is very important. The BMW X5 has a ground clearance of approximately 214mm.
Adaptive Air Suspension: The best part is that you can increase or decrease the car's height with a button. This helps the car go over big speed breakers without any damage.

5. High-Tech Interior

The interior of the X5 feels like a private jet. It features the BMW Curved Display, which combines the driver's screen and the touch screen into one beautiful piece of glass.

  • Harman Kardon Sound: Amazing music quality with 16 speakers.
  • Panoramic Sunroof: A large glass roof that makes the cabin feel open and airy.
  • Four-Zone Climate Control: Different temperatures for all passengers.

6. Fuel Efficiency (Mileage)

Luxury SUVs are not known for high mileage, but the X5 is decent for its size:

Petrol Mileage:8 to 11 kmpl
Diesel Mileage:11 to 14 kmpl

8. Maintenance and Service

BMW offers "BSI" (Service Packages) that cover all service costs for 3 to 10 years. For a car like the X5, we recommend taking a 5-year package to stay worry-free from high service bills.

9. Final Verdict: Should You Buy?

PROS:
Powerful engines, great road presence, high-tech interior, and very safe for the family.
CONS:
Expensive maintenance after warranty, and no 7-seater option in India.
