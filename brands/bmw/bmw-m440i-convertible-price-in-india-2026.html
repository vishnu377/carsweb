2. Engine: The Heart of a Beast (B58)

The 3.0L Inline-6 engine, known as the B58, is often called the best engine BMW has ever made. It’s reliable, smooth, and incredibly powerful.

  • Horsepower: 374 bhp - 386 bhp (Depends on fuel quality)
  • Torque: 500 Nm (Available from 1,900 RPM)
  • 0-100 km/h: Just 4.5 Seconds
  • Top Speed: 250 km/h (Limited)

3. The Dramatic Soft-Top Mechanism

Unlike the old hard-tops, the 2026 M440i uses a fabric soft-top. It's 40% lighter, which helps the car handle better. How fast is it? You can open it in 18 seconds while driving at 50 km/h. Imagine the looks you'll get at the red light!

4. Ground Clearance: The Biggest Fear

India is famous for big speed breakers. The M440i has a ground clearance of approx 130mm.
The Truth: You will have to be very careful. If you have 4 people in the car, you MUST drive slowly over bumps to avoid scraping the belly.

8. Driving Modes: How it feels?

You get four modes: Eco Pro, Comfort, Sport, and Sport Plus. In Sport Plus, the exhaust makes "pops and bangs" sounds that are music to any car guy's ears.

9. Safety Features (Safety First!)

BMW hasn't skipped safety. Even without a metal roof, you are safe:

  • Rollover Protection: Hidden bars that pop up in 0.1s during an accident.
  • ADSA Level 2: Lane keep assist and emergency braking.
  • Parking Assistant Plus: 360-degree cameras to help in tight spots.

10. Practicality & Boot Space

Let's be real—the boot space is small. With the roof down, you can only fit two small bags. This car is for road trips with a partner, not for a family shopping trip.

12. Ownership & Service Cost

BMW offers "BSI" (BMW Service Inclusive) packages. For the M440i, expect to pay around ₹1.5 Lakh for a 3-year package. This covers all oils, filters, and labor.
