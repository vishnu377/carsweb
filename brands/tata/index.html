About Tata Motors

Tata Motors is part of the legendary Tata Group and has become a household name in India for safety and reliability. They are the pioneers of the EV revolution in India, commanding over 70% market share in the electric passenger vehicle segment. With their "Impact 2.0" design language, Tata cars like Harrier and Safari have set new benchmarks for premium SUVs.
