Tata Sierra EV 2026: The Iconic Legend Returns in Electric Avatar

By: Auto News Editorial Last Updated: Dec 31, 2025

Tata Motors is reviving the legendary Sierra nameplate as a premium electric SUV, blending nostalgia with cutting-edge technology.

The upcoming Tata Sierra EV made headlines at the Auto Expo with its "Alpine windows" and retro-modern silhouette. Positioned above the Curvv EV, this SUV aims to redefine the mid-size electric segment in India with superior range and luxury features.

Exterior & Design

The production-spec Sierra EV retains the boxy, upright stance of the original 90s model. Key highlights include a closed-off front grille, a full-width LED DRL bar, flush-fitting door handles, and massive 19-inch diamond-cut alloy wheels. The signature rear glass design remains a tribute to the classic Sierra heritage.

Futuristic 3-Screen Interior

The cabin is a tech-lover's paradise. It features a minimalist dashboard with a triple-screen setup: a digital driver display, a massive central infotainment unit, and a dedicated entertainment screen for the co-passenger. Luxury additions include a panoramic sunroof, 12-speaker JBL audio with Dolby Atmos, and ventilated seats.

Expected Launch & Price

The Tata Sierra EV is expected to launch in India by January 2026. While official prices are yet to be announced, estimates suggest a starting price of ₹15 Lakh to ₹25 Lakh (ex-showroom), depending on the battery size and trim level.

Main Competitors

Upon launch, the Sierra EV will face tough competition from:
